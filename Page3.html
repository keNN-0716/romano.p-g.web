Kendal Jenner's Styles

Kendall Jenner was frst thrust into the public eye 16 years ago, as the quiet, tomboyish 11-year-old younger sister on Keeping Up With the Kardashians. But in 2014, the second-youngest member of the Kar-Jenner dynasty struck out to forge her own path as a model. Since her runway debut for Marc Jacobs’ fall 2014 show, Jenner has become a ubiquitous presence, not only on our TV screens, but on the runway and in magazines, too. With such a jam-packed schedule, the budding model became a regular street style subject, as she bounced from show to show.

Early photos captured Jenner’s style at the beginning of its evolution. Even at 19, she shows the same fashion DNA that she has today: a love of simplicity. As she’s become more entrenched in the fashion world, Jenner’s interest in fashion history broadened, as well as her wardrobe. These days, whether she’s rocking head-to-toe The Row, showing of her latest Phoebe Philo purchase, or pulling from her Alaïa archive, she’s become one of our favorite street style stars.
